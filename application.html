Eligibility

  • Must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident (required by NSF).
  • Must be currently enrolled as an undergraduate student with strong academic record.
  • Must have completed at least 1 year of college coursework or equivalent.
  • Must attend daily activities in person at the UNT Discovery Park during the 10-week program.

Application Requirements

  • Academic Transcripts. Unofficial ones are fine.
  • Resume or Curriculum Vitae (CV).
  • Contact information for a reference who can comment on your academic preparation in college and any research experiences, community involvement, ect.

How to Apply

Please submit your application using the link below. To ensure the security of uploaded files, a Google account login is required.

https://forms.gle/femQmgdfrTk6ku2p6

Stipend and Benefits

  • $7,000 stipend.
  • Housing covered.
  • Travel and meal allowance.
  • All types of income are taxable.

Important Dates

  • April 30, 2025: Application deadline.
  • May 9, 2025: Notification of acceptance. Once an offer of acceptance has been extended to an applicant, a confirmation must be received within one week of the offer date.
  • May 19, 2025: REU program starting date.
  • July 25, 2025: REU program ending date.
